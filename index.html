📊 Market Reliability Scoring Method

Each model gets a Market Reliability score (1–5) derived from UK/Ireland surveys:

  • What Car? 2025 Brand Score (29,697 owners, 199 models ≤5 yrs old) — primary source
  • Honest John Satisfaction Index 2025 (~5,000 UK owners) — model-level where available (Lexus NX 98%, Skoda Karoq 97.7%, etc.)
  • CarSpot Ireland 2025 — used car reliability for Irish context (NCT pass rates, parts availability)
  • Normalization: Brand % → 1–5 scale: ≥95%=5, 93–94.9%=4, 91–92.9%=3, 89–90.9%=2, <89%=1
  • Model override: If specific model has Honest John data, that overrides brand average
